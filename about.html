My Journey

About Me

I am an MCA graduate from Nirmala College, Muvattupuzha (2023–2025), with a strong foundation in computer science and practical expertise in Data Transmission, Networking Systems, Software Engineering, Object Modeling, and Machine Learning. Prior to this, I completed my BCA at NSS College, Rajakumari (2020–2023), where I built solid programming and software development skills. Throughout my academic journey, I have engaged in hands-on projects and earned certifications in Full Stack Web Development and Cybersecurity Fundamentals, which have strengthened my ability to create secure, scalable, and efficient applications.

Among my notable projects is the Agro Pesticide Management System, a PHP-based web application designed to streamline pesticide product data, stock, and distribution for agricultural use.

My Approach

Software should be simple, scalable, and impactful. I prioritize thoughtful design, reliable code, and user-centric solutions over flashy features. Clear communication, constructive feedback, and collaboration are central to my process, ensuring projects meet both technical requirements and business goals.

Why Collaborate With Me

I bring a blend of technical proficiency and a commitment to quality, delivering maintainable code that drives results. Whether working solo or within a team, I focus on creating applications that are efficient, scalable, and aligned with user needs.

Personal Interests

Beyond coding, I’m an avid football enthusiast—both playing and watching—and find inspiration in problem-solving, whether on the field or at the keyboard. I actively engage with the tech community through conference presentations and open-source contributions.
